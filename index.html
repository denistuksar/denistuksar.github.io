GeoApp
C#, MySQL

GeoApp - C#, MySQL

GeoApp is an application that aims to facilitate the process of purchasing and servicing geodetic devices and equipment. It is possible to log in to the system as a customer, administrator or employee.

Customers can view and manage the information about servicing, assessments and other services offered by the company and have the ability to order geodetic devices and equipment. Employees can insert the devices and equipment into the database and update them, and the administrator has the ability to edit user privileges.

Airports - MySQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
